The Journey of Psychology

Discover the minds and milestones that shaped the understanding of human behavior.

Psychology Timeline

  • 1879 - Wilhelm Wundt establishes the first experimental psychology lab in Leipzig, Germany.
  • 1900 - Sigmund Freud publishes *The Interpretation of Dreams*, founding psychoanalysis.
  • 1913 - John B. Watson promotes behaviorism.
  • 1950s - Humanistic psychology rises with Carl Rogers and Abraham Maslow.
  • 1960s - Cognitive psychology emerges as a dominant paradigm.
  • Present - Psychology integrates neuroscience, social sciences, and technology.

Meet the Pioneers

Sigmund Freud

The father of psychoanalysis, exploring the unconscious mind and the significance of dreams.

Carl Jung

Introduced archetypes and the collective unconscious, expanding analytical psychology.

Carl Rogers

Championed humanistic psychology and the client-centered therapeutic approach.
